We provide past data from July 2008 onwards in an hourly interval.

Historical weather could be provided in 2 ways:-

1. First Option: If you have a location list then we will generate past data in CSV/XML/JSON format for you and you only need to pay once for the data. Pricing is based on the number of locations and number of days of weather data. Please get in touch to get a price quote.

2. Second Option: We will provide you with our History Weather API and you could get data by Postcode/Zipcode, Lat/Lon, IP address, city name and airport code. Learn more here https://developer.worldweatheronline.com/api/historical-weather-api.aspx
